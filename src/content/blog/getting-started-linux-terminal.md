If you're new to Linux, start with these commands:

- `pwd` to print your current working directory.
- `ls -la` to inspect files, including hidden ones.
- `cd` to navigate directories quickly.
- `man <command>` to open the manual page for a command.

Spend 15 minutes a day in the terminal and you'll level up fast.
