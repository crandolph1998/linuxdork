Immich is one of the rare self-hosted apps that makes people immediately ask, "wait, you host this yourself?" That reaction matters because it means the app is doing enough polish work that the infrastructure disappears.

## Where it shines

Photo backup is the main reason to run it, but browsing, search, and timeline views are what make it feel sticky. If those parts are clumsy, people stop trusting the platform.

## Operational notes

```bash
Give it fast storage.
Watch backup strategy before upgrades.
Read release notes instead of blindly pulling latest.
```

For anyone trying to reduce dependence on commercial photo platforms, Immich is one of the strongest self-hosted options available right now.
